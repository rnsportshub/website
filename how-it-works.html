Ordering Guide

HOW IT
WORKS

Ordering from RN Sports Hub is fast and simple. Follow these steps to get your gear delivered straight to your door — pay via UPI or choose Cash on Delivery.

STEPS
💳 UPI Payment

Pay instantly using GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, or BHIM. Upload screenshot to confirm order.

🚚 Cash on Delivery

Prefer to pay when your order arrives? COD is available. Simply select it at checkout.

Step by Step
1
🛍️
Select Your Product

Browse jerseys, studs, and accessories on our shop. Choose your size and add to cart.

2
📦
Fill Your Address

Enter your delivery details — name, phone, full address, and pincode. City & state auto-fill from PIN code.

3
💳
Choose Payment

Select UPI (scan QR or use UPI ID) or Cash on Delivery to pay when your order arrives.

4
📸
Confirm Your Order

For UPI — upload your payment screenshot. For COD — your order is placed directly. Status: Confirmed ✓

5
✅
Order Dispatched

Your order is packed and dispatched. Shipping & tracking details are shared via WhatsApp within 24 hours.

For UPI Orders — What To Share on WhatsApp
✓Order ID: So our team can match your payment to the right order.
✓Payment Screenshot: A clear screenshot showing the completed UPI transaction.
✓Any Clarification: Sizing notes, address updates, or special requests.
For COD Orders
✓No advance payment needed — pay the delivery agent when the parcel arrives.
✓Ensure your phone number is active so the courier can reach you at delivery.
✓Keep the exact amount ready to make the handover smooth and quick.
✓Contact us on WhatsApp for any order updates or tracking queries.

When Does Shipping Start?

Shipping starts once the order is officially confirmed — after UPI verification or upon COD placement. Tracking details follow within 24 hours.
