RN Sports Hub — Exchange Policy
⚠ No Refund. No Return. Exchange Only.

We do not offer refunds or returns under any circumstances. Please review all product details, size charts, and images carefully before placing your order. Exchange requests may be considered under specific conditions outlined below.

No Refund Policy

RN Sports Hub does not issue refunds under any circumstances, including change of mind, incorrect size selection, delayed delivery, or personal preference. By placing an order, you acknowledge that the transaction is final and no monetary refund will be provided.

All UPI payments and COD payments made towards your order are non-refundable once the order is confirmed and dispatched. For queries, contact us on WhatsApp.

No Return Policy

RN Sports Hub does not accept product returns for any reason once an order has been dispatched. All products are carefully verified before shipping. We encourage customers to review size charts, product descriptions, and images thoroughly before purchasing.

✓ Exchange Policy

Exchange is Available

Unlike returns or refunds, we do offer exchanges under eligible conditions. Read below to understand how exchanges work at RN Sports Hub.

Exchange requests will be considered on a case-by-case basis. To be eligible for an exchange, the following conditions must be met:

  • The product must be unused, unwashed, and in its original condition with all tags intact.
  • The exchange request must be raised within 48 hours of receiving the product.
  • The product must not show signs of use, damage, or alteration of any kind.
  • The customer must provide clear unboxing video or photographic proof of the issue.
  • Exchange is only for the same product in a different size, or for a product of equal or lesser value.

Exchanges will not be processed for the following reasons:

  • Personal preference or change of mind.
  • Incorrect size selected by the customer (please use the size chart before ordering).
  • Products that have been used, washed, or altered.
  • Products missing original tags or packaging.
  • Requests raised after 48 hours of delivery.
