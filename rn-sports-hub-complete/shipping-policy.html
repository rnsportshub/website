Shipping & Dispatch Policy

We aim to keep delivery timelines clear and reliable. Every order is reviewed before it moves to packing and dispatch, helping reduce errors and improve delivery accuracy.

Order Processing Time

Orders are reviewed and prepared after payment is confirmed. In most cases, verified orders are processed and dispatched within 1 to 3 working days from the time payment is confirmed — whether via UPI or Cash on Delivery placement.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

✅ COD is Available

RN Sports Hub accepts Cash on Delivery. You can pay in cash to the delivery agent when your parcel arrives at your doorstep. No advance payment needed for COD orders.

For COD orders, ensure your phone number is active and the exact amount is ready for smooth delivery. Orders will be dispatched after placement without any additional verification steps.

Dispatch After UPI Payment Verification

For UPI orders, dispatch begins only after your payment has been verified by our team. If payment confirmation or screenshot clarification is pending, dispatch may be delayed until verification is complete. Share your payment screenshot on WhatsApp to speed up confirmation.

Delivery Timeline

Typical delivery timelines range from 4 to 7 working days after dispatch, depending on your location and courier routing. Metro cities may receive orders faster, while remote or high-load regions may take longer.

  • Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, etc.): 2–4 working days
  • Tier-2 & Tier-3 cities: 4–6 working days
  • Remote or high-load PIN codes: 5–8 working days

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, tracking details will be shared via WhatsApp. You can use the tracking link or AWB number to monitor your shipment in real time through the courier's website.

You can also visit our Track Order page for self-serve tracking.

Incorrect Address or Contact Details

Please ensure that your shipping address, phone number, and all delivery details are correct when placing your order. RN Sports Hub is not responsible for failed or returned deliveries caused by incomplete or incorrect information submitted at checkout.

If you need to update your address or contact info, please WhatsApp us immediately after placing your order — before dispatch.

Exchange Policy

We do not offer refunds or returns, but we do accept exchange requests under specific conditions. Please review our full Exchange Policy for complete details and eligibility criteria.
